Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 04, 2025

analyticsgtm (“the App”) helps Shopify merchants install Google Tag Manager (GTM), configure a consistent dataLayer, and enable Shopify Customer Events for checkout attribution. We keep data collection to the minimum required to perform these functions and do not sell personal information.

Information We Access

  • Theme files (read/write) to insert GTM tags and a small dataLayer snippet.
  • Web Pixel / Customer Events permissions to install a custom pixel you control.
  • Shop metadata (shop domain, theme id) to operate the install flow.

We do not collect or store your customers’ personal information (PII) on our servers.

How We Use Information

Any analytics events generated by the dataLayer are sent directly to the destinations you configure (e.g., Google Tag Manager/Analytics). Our servers are used only to render the installer UI and to perform one‑time theme/pixel updates at your request.

Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. We may use standard cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting) purely to run the service.

Data Retention

We store only minimal operational logs (e.g., install time, shop domain) for troubleshooting and security. We do not store customer event payloads. You can request deletion of operational data at any time.

Security

  • All communication occurs over HTTPS.
  • No customer PII is persisted by us.
  • Theme and pixel changes are performed with your Admin API token; you can revoke access at any time.

Your Controls

  • Disable or uninstall the app to revoke access.
  • Remove the injected snippet or GTM tags from your theme at any time.
  • Request deletion of operational records by contacting us.

International Transfers

Your store data may be processed on servers located outside your jurisdiction by our hosting provider. We rely on standard contractual safeguards with our sub‑processors.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. We will adjust the “Last updated” date above and, where required, notify you in the app.
